Nick Knight

The fashion's imagemaking visionary

— Devon Aoki for Alexander McQueen (1997).


Nick Knight is among the world’s most influential and visionary photographers, and founder and director of award-winning fashion website SHOWstudio.com. As a fashion photographer, he has consistently challenged conventional notions of beauty and is fêted for his groundbreaking creative collaborations with leading designers including Yohji Yamamoto, John Galliano, Alexander McQueen.


  • 1958

    Nick Knight was born on 24 November 1958 in Hammersmith, London. He studied photography at Bournemouth and Poole Collge of Art and Design.

  • 1982

    The skinheead book comes out documenting the subculture inspired by rudeboy culture and reacting to Hippie movement of 60s and 70s.

  • 1986

    The work of Nick Knight, catches Marc Ascoli's eye, commisioning him to take pictures for Yohji Yamamoto. The pioneering work sets the tone of image-making for years the come. Subtle portrayal of Yohji's collection using lights and shadows results in a great portrayal of the collection.

  • 1992

    A year long break from fashion industry results in a collaboration with the Natural History Museum and David Chipperfield. An exhibition examining the relationship betweeen the humans and nature lasts 5 years. Moreover, Nick's fascination with flowers has morphed into photographing flowers in his garden. An act of careful composition, but a quick result, thanks to his tool:an iPhone

  • 2000

    SHOWstudio started its journey in November 2000, innovative and ground-breaking projects have defined the manner in which fashion is presented via the Internet. Working with the latest technology SHOWstudio broadcasts live from catwalk shows and fashion shoots, allowing an international audience instant and unparalleled access to the previously closed world of high fashion

  • 2001

    Directs a music video for Bjork calleed "Pagan Poetry."

  • 2016

    He was commissioned to shoot official portraits of Queen Elizabeth and Prince Charles for the Queen's 90th birthday."

  • 2019

    After collaborating previously on music video Bound 2, Kanye West asked Nick to direct a short film for his upcoming album Jesus is King."
